After being at the Iowa Softball Hall of Fame induction ceremony yesterday, 
it brought back memories of the 1995 induction of Al Rausch, Gregg Bosch, 
Mike Palleson, umpire Bob Hoskins, and me. Highlight of my career! I played 
with and against Al, Gregg & the Hammer and it was such an honor to be inducted 
with 3 of the best players I ever saw. Incredible memory! This is an article 
that the Cedar Rapids newspaper did before the ceremony.

- Denny Linderbaum
